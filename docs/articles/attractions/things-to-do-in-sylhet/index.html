Sylhet is a region in Bangladesh known for its natural beauty, tea gardens, and cultural heritage. Here's a list of things to do and places to visit in Sylhet:

  1. Jaflong and Dawki River: Explore the breathtaking landscapes, lush tea gardens, and the Dawki River, which forms the border between Bangladesh and India.
  2. Srimangal: Visit the "Tea Capital" of Bangladesh, known for its numerous tea estates and the Lawachara National Park, which is home to diverse wildlife.
  3. Ratargul Swamp Forest: Experience a unique boat ride in one of the few swamp forests in Bangladesh, offering a different kind of natural beauty.
  4. Lalakhal: Take a boat ride on the clear blue waters of the Lalakhal river, surrounded by green hills and tea gardens.
  5. Ratnodweep: Explore the serene Ratnodweep Island in the middle of the Surma River, where you can relax and enjoy the tranquility.
  6. Shahi Eidgah: Visit this historic mosque and open prayer ground, known for its impressive architecture and significance in the region.
  7. Shrine of Hazrat Shah Jalal: Pay your respects at the mausoleum of Hazrat Shah Jalal, a revered Sufi saint, and explore the surrounding area.
  8. Shrine of Hazrat Shah Paran: Another important Sufi shrine, dedicated to Hazrat Shah Paran, is worth a visit for its spiritual atmosphere.
  9. Sylhet Museum: Learn about the history, culture, and heritage of the region through various artifacts and exhibits.
  10. Khadimnagar National Park: Enjoy the natural beauty and wildlife of this national park, home to various species of plants, birds, and animals.
  11. Adventure Sports: If you're into adventure, you can engage in activities like rock climbing, trekking, and hiking in the hilly terrain.
  12. Ratargul Swamp Forest: Explore this unique swamp forest and enjoy boat rides amidst the lush greenery and diverse flora and fauna.
